About MAThe

Research project of the Multidisciplinary Media & Mediated Communication Research Group (M3C)

The MAThe project is a research project of the Multidisciplinary Media & Mediated Communication Research Group (M3C) of the Aristotle University of Thessaloniki, introducing computational and gamification approaches in the fight against misinformation.

Our Mission

To introduce computational and gamification approaches in the fight against misinformation through advanced text analysis and authentication techniques.
